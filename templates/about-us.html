Let me tell you our story ...

          Revvie

They say, ‘Your first car is your first love’. Compatibility and trust is the foundation of any relationship. Buying your first car can be really tricky and overwhelming, and lack of proper research can lead you into a really toxic & complicated relationship. The car has to perfectly align with an individual’s requirements for a long term partnership and this can always be one of the toughest choices in his/her life. There is so much information on the internet that your mind is inundated and the decision making becomes really difficult. But things will be so much easier if someone you trust holds your hand and guides you through this ordeal. ‘AggieCarTales.com’ is a one-stop solution for all the car choosing problems. It offers honest reviews and advice from a community that you can trust with all your heart, on which car might be the best for you. This website will also have a mobile-friendly interface along with a desktop/laptop version in order to make it more accessible for Aggies during any time of the day. This will provide a platform for aggies to guide fellow aggies and foster a stronger bond through the community.
